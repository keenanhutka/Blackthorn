Core Philosophy
All sentient beings in the Shattered Principalities are described using a 1–100 spectrum for key physical and sensory traits. These numbers do not represent absolute measurements but rather a character’s position on a race- and gender-specific range. A score of 1 is the minimum possible for that race/gender, while 100 is the maximum. This allows fine-grained differentiation while keeping mechanics consistent across species.
Genital size and orifice capacity use a simplified 1–7 relative scale (detailed below) to make compatibility fast and intuitive while preserving race-based erotic tension. Sexual Corruption (0–100) is a new tracked stat that allows highly corrupt NPCs to safely accommodate larger sizes without magic or alchemy. All other traits remain on the 1–100 spectrum.
Body traits directly influence seduction rolls, BDSM outcomes, slave market value, crafting of custom items (e.g., restraints or dildos), and social perception. Poor management of traits like Hygiene or Scent can cause Defiance spikes, reputation damage, or scene failure.

CAMPAIGN_MILESTONE_GUIDE - Version 1.5 (Grimdark Erotic Fantasy, 70 AE - Shattered Principalities)

Core Philosophy
This guide provides a clear, modular campaign arc for the Shattered Principalities, designed to take a single low-resource player character (or small group) from street-level survival to generational empire-building. The structure leverages all core systems — especially Time_System.txt v1.1 for aging and generational consequences, Social_Advancement.txt v1.5 for tier progression, Random_Events_Table.txt v1.14 for volatile daily life, Slavery_and_Defiance_Core_Mechanics, Sex_BDSM_System, Estate_Management_and_Daily_Life, and the low-resource Starter_Adventures.

Campaigns emphasize consequence, time passage, and legacy. Even “successful” choices carry grimdark costs: aging weakens the owner, slaves mature and develop their own Defiance, bloodlines create new assets or liabilities, and visible decline can trigger reputation bleed or rebellion chains. Sessions can be run weekly or with time-skips (weeks/months/years) using Time_System monthly checks.

Campaign Structure Overview (Approximate Session Count for 2–4 hour sessions)
- Phase 1: Street Survival & First Assets (Sessions 1–20, ~0–12 in-game months)
- Phase 2: Estate Foundations & Local Power (Sessions 21–50, ~1–4 in-game years)
- Phase 3: Regional Influence & Political Maneuvering (Sessions 51–100, ~4–12 in-game years)
- Phase 4: Generational Legacy & Empire Building (Sessions 101+, 12+ in-game years)

Each phase includes milestone goals, recommended XP awards (feeding into Social_Advancement), key system touchpoints, sample hooks (drawing from Starter_Adventures, Random_Events, and Random_NPC_Generator), and Time_System aging checkpoints.

Phase 1: Street Survival & First Assets (Social Standing Tier 0–1)
Goal: Survive the slums, acquire your first slave or workshop tool, establish basic income, and avoid immediate capture or starvation while learning the brutal basics of ownership and consequence.

Key Milestones
- Acquire first low-value slave or small asset through capture, black-market purchase, or opportunistic scavenging (500 XP)
- Complete 1–2 low-risk jobs or raids (scavenging, minor smuggling, or petty theft) without major Defiance incidents or detection (300–600 XP each)
- Survive your first public scandal or hygiene-related social failure (e.g., appearing in rags with a foul-scented slave in public) and recover through basic aftercare or quick fashion upgrade (400 XP)
- Successfully manage your first Defiance spike with a basic motivation or restraint scene, learning the core loop of control and consequence (300 XP)
- Reach Social Standing Tier 1 (3000 XP total + meet 2 advancement methods)

Time_System Checkpoint: First 1–3 Monthly Aging Checks. Minimal visible change for the PC, but poor street conditions can cause early Hygiene/Scent penalties or minor Body trait wear on any acquired slaves. First taste of daily Defiance drift under harsh conditions. Players should feel the pressure of time even in the opening sessions — every wasted day increases drift and risk.

Phase 2: Estate Foundations & Local Power (Social Standing Tier 1–2)
Goal: Secure a small workshop or holding, stabilize 2–4 slaves, generate consistent income, begin managing daily routines, and establish a foothold reputation in one settlement while feeling the weight of ongoing time pressure.

Key Milestones
- Acquire and upgrade first basic property (Workshop or Small Holding) (800–1500 XP)

- Host first private BDSM gathering or successful crafting commission with visible fashion/status elements (600–1000 XP)
- Survive or capitalize on your first seasonal event or festival complication (500 XP)
- Reach Social Standing Tier 2 (additional 3000 XP + meet 2 advancement methods)

Longevity potions (Alchemy_Consumables_and_Potions v1.5) or minor rituals become relevant for the first time. Generational planning begins if any breeding occurs; early offspring conception risks add tension.

- Seasonal festival event (Festival Events Table) that risks public scandal or reputation gain, amplified by your emerging luxury items
- First Random_Events_Table aging-modulated complication (e.g., heat wave increasing Defiance drift during early estate management)
- First multi-slave motivation scene where poor Tier 1 fashion causes partial failure and forces quick upgrades

Recommended Systems Focus

Phase 3: Regional Influence & Political Maneuvering (Social Standing Tier 2–4)
Goal: Expand holdings across multiple districts or baronies, forge multi-faction alliances, manage larger slave populations (8+), influence baronial politics, navigate growing personal and generational costs of aging, and begin positioning yourself as a regional power while confronting the first real signs of long-term decline.

Key Milestones

- Successfully resolve 1–2 Major Rebellion Chains or Festival events without catastrophic loss or major reputation bleed (1200–1500 XP each)
- Complete high-value multi-skill commissions or breeding contracts that become regional status symbols (especially those involving luxury fashion, enchanted restraints, or signature bloodlines) (1500–2000 XP)
- Survive or exploit a major cross-faction scandal tied to your growing visibility and aging appearance (e.g., a public display where reduced Attractiveness or scent control causes complications) (1000 XP)
- Orchestrate or survive at least one political intrigue that shifts local power dynamics in your favor (1200 XP)
- Reach Social Standing Tier 3 or 4 (8000–15,000 XP + meet 3–4 advancement methods)

Time_System Checkpoint: 2–5 in-game years of Monthly Aging Checks. PC and senior slaves show noticeable aging (potential Body trait loss, reduced fertility, social perception penalties). Longevity treatments or necromantic options carry increasing backlash risk. First offspring from breeding programs reach maturity (use Random_NPC_Generator + NPC_System), introducing new NPCs with inherited traits, their own Defiance, and potential ambitions or value as assets. - Rival faction attempts to expose your black-market ties or poor longevity management, forcing a high-stakes public BDSM demonstration or political maneuver

Recommended Systems Focus

Phase 4: Generational Legacy & Empire Building (Social Standing Tier 4–5)
Goal: Establish a lasting bloodline or empire, manage multi-generational estates, shape baronial power, confront the realities of personal aging and succession, and decide what kind of legacy you will leave as time inevitably catches up.

Key Milestones
- Create and stabilize a signature bloodline with desirable inherited traits across 2–3 generations (2000+ XP)
- Shift regional power balance (e.g., elevate one church or crush a rival house through political, erotic, or economic means) (3000 XP)
- Successfully hand off major responsibilities to mature offspring or loyal lieutenants while managing your own visible aging decline (1500–2500 XP)
- Reach Social Standing Tier 5 (25,000 XP + meet all advancement methods)
- Resolve a major succession crisis or rebellion involving your own bloodline (2000 XP)
- Establish at least one legendary status symbol (crafted item, signature bloodline, or grand festival) that outlives your personal prime (2500 XP)

Time_System Checkpoint: 5+ in-game years with frequent Monthly Aging Checks. The PC may require repeated longevity interventions with compounding grimdark costs (mutations, accelerated backlash, Defiance inheritance issues to offspring). Generational hand-off becomes central — offspring age into playable roles or key NPCs with their own Body traits, Defiance, ambitions, and potential to either strengthen or undermine your empire. Visible decline in the founder can trigger reputation bleed, slave unrest, or rival challenges. Late-game seasons and lunar cycles dramatically amplify event outcomes and daily management difficulty.

- Grand breeding festival or legendary crafting project that cements your legacy while highlighting aging contrasts with younger generations
- Final confrontation with a long-term rival whose aging mirrors your own, possibly involving parallel longevity struggles or competing bloodlines
- Multi-generational Random_Events_Table complication during a major estate or baronial event, where aging effects dramatically shift outcomes (e.g., a harsh winter exposing founder weakness)
