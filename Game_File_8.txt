MID_TIER_ADVENTURE_HOOKS - Version 1.1 (Grimdark Erotic Fantasy, 70 AE - Shattered Principalities)
Core Philosophy
These mid-tier adventure hooks are designed for Sessions 004–008, when the player has moved beyond absolute poverty and now possesses:
- 1–2 basic slaves or followers
- A small workshop, rented room, or modest property
- Social Standing Tier 1–2
- Basic crafting tools and some reputation
They build directly on the low-resource Starter Adventures and Black Market & Crime Scenarios, introducing first estate upgrades, minor noble commissions, faction intrigue, and larger-scale Defiance management while still keeping consequences heavy and resources limited.
Each hook includes:
- Premise and required assets
- Pre-generated NPCs (using Random_NPC_Generator v1.4)
- Key scenes with Combat, BDSM, Magic, Fashion/Status (LUXURY_FASHION_AND_STATUS v1.2), and Defiance integration
- Defiance trackers where relevant
- Multiple endings with XP and Social Advancement rewards

Hook 1: The First Workshop Commission
Premise: A minor guild artisan offers the PC their first real crafting commission — custom restraints for a noble’s new pleasure slave — but the materials are expensive and the deadline tight.

  Body Traits: Attractiveness 78, Foot Attractiveness 72

- Commission Slave – Race: Human, Role: Experienced Slave, Defiance: 52, Sexual Corruption: 45

1. Negotiation & Fashion Display (Social_Advancement + LUXURY_FASHION_AND_STATUS): Present your follower in the best available clothing/scent to impress the client.
2. Crafting Challenge (Crafting_System): Multi-skill project under time pressure. Failure risks damaged reputation or partial payment.
3. Delivery & Test Scene: Deliver the restraints and demonstrate them in a private BDSM motivation scene.
Defiance Tracker (Commission Slave)
