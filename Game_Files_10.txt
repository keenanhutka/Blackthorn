CHARACTER CREATION GUIDELINES
================================================================
Core Philosophy
Characters in the Shattered Principalities are ambitious individuals seeking to rise through ownership, erotic mastery, crafting skill, and political maneuvering. Creation focuses on making characters that feel part of the grimdark world — where power is intoxicating but fragile, pleasure is laced with risk, and every decision carries consequences.
Step 1: Choose Race
Use Races_Reference.txt v1.6 for full racial details, including maturity ages and the mandatory Adult trait. Each race has three Racial Feats/Heritage options (choose 1 at character creation unless GM allows more).
- Human – Versatile baseline. Highly adaptable in all social and erotic contexts. Balanced Body traits make them excellent starting characters for players who want flexibility in seduction, labor, or crafting paths.
  **Racial Feats:** Adaptive (once per session, reroll any failed skill check), Cosmopolitan (+1 to any social skill), Quick Learner (gain +10 skill ranks in one non-erotic skill at creation).
- Orc – Strong, intimidating, high endurance. Naturally muscular builds and high baseline Defiance make them prized as guards or heavy laborers, but they respond best to raw physical dominance and honor-based training.
  **Racial Feats:** Brutal Strength (+2 to melee damage and intimidation), Honor-Bound (advantage on saves vs fear or mind control when protecting allies), Iron Constitution (+2 to Constitution checks and resistance to fatigue during long sessions).
- Elf – Graceful, high Attractiveness, elegant seduction. Naturally high General Attractiveness and delicate Scent make them premium pleasure slaves or diplomats; they excel at teasing, edging, and refined humiliation scenes but resent crude treatment.
  **Racial Feats:** Graceful Tease (+3 to Seduction and edging scenes), Elegant Resilience (resistance to pain-based Defiance spikes), Ethereal Grace (+2 to Dexterity and Foot Attractiveness checks).
- Dwarf – Sturdy, excellent crafting aptitude. Compact and resilient; they value endurance testing, possessive marking, and well-crafted restraints. Strong natural Hygiene resilience but can become stubborn if disrespected.
  **Racial Feats:** Forge-Blood (+3 to metalwork and blacksmithing), Stubborn Will (advantage on saves vs Domination or mind effects), Enduring Frame (+2 to Constitution and resistance to environmental penalties).
- Goblin – Small, inventive, chaotic. Highly expressive and opportunistic; they thrive in group play, trickery, and experimental toys. High energy but short attention spans make them excellent for chaotic or gadget-focused erotic scenes.
  **Racial Feats:** Gadgeteer (+2 to Engineering and experimental crafting), Chaotic Energy (once per session, gain advantage on a chaotic or trickery-based roll), Swarm Tactics (+2 to group combat or group erotic scenes).
- Gnome – Tiny, mechanical ingenuity. Clever and detail-oriented; they excel at puzzle-like restraints, intricate sensation play, and inventive erotic devices.
  **Racial Feats:** Puzzle-Mind (+3 to intricate gadgets and traps), Tiny Ingenuity (advantage on checks involving small or precise mechanisms), Inventive Mind (+2 to Alchemy and experimental potion crafting).
- Dryad – Nature-tied, high Scent and organic beauty. Strongly linked to natural cycles; they respond exceptionally well to living-vine bondage, floral scent play, and seasonal fertility rites. Neglect causes rapid withering.
  **Racial Feats:** Living Vine Affinity (+3 to nature-infused Bondage and sensory scenes), Grove Resilience (faster recovery from damage when near nature), Seasonal Bloom (+2 to Scent and General Attractiveness during fertile cycles).
- Giant – Massive, overwhelming physical presence. Require specially crafted massive-scale gear. Size-difference play is central; they are excellent dominants or challenging submissives but demand significant resources and reinforced facilities.
  **Racial Feats:** Crushing Might (+3 to grapple and intimidation), Towering Presence (advantage on intimidation against smaller creatures), Massive Endurance (+2 to Constitution and resistance to size-based penalties).
All characters must possess the Adult trait before any erotic, breeding, or BDSM mechanics can apply (see Races_Reference.txt for maturity ages).
Step 2: Ability Scores
Roll or assign using one of these methods (GM choice):
- Standard Array: 15, 14, 13, 12, 10, 8
- Point Buy (27 points)
- Roll 4d6 drop lowest (6 times)
Assign to: Strength, Dexterity, Constitution, Intelligence, Wisdom, Charisma.
Step 3: Determine Background & Social Standing
Choose a starting background that fits the world (examples below). This sets starting Social Standing tier, starting gold, and basic equipment.
Background Examples
- Escaped Slave / Street Urchin (Tier 0) – High Defiance knowledge, street smarts. Starting gold: 2d10 gp.
- Apprentice Crafter (Tier 1) – Basic crafting skill. Starting gold: 4d10 gp + basic tools.
- Minor Merchant / Caravan Guard (Tier 1–2) – Trade knowledge. Starting gold: 6d10 gp + simple weapon.
- Indentured Overseer (Tier 2) – Experience managing slaves. Starting gold: 8d10 gp.
- Disgraced Noble Scion (Tier 2) – Some reputation but heavy debts. Starting gold: 10d10 gp.
Starting Social Standing is usually Tier 0 or 1. Higher starting tiers require GM approval and significant backstory justification.
Step 4: Choose Class Archetype
Class Archetypes define your primary path to power. They are not rigid classes but flexible roles that emphasize different strengths in this grimdark world of ownership, erotic control, and exploitation. Choose one archetype and gain its core benefits and suggested skills. Racial synergies provide additional bonuses when the archetype aligns with a race’s natural strengths.
**Dominant Owner / Slaver Archetype**
Focus: Mastery of slavery, Defiance management, and erotic dominance.
Core Benefits: +2 to all Domination and Intimidation checks. +3 bonus to BDSM motivation scene rolls when reducing Defiance. Easier access to high-value slaves and training facilities. Once per session, gain advantage on a Defiance reduction roll after a successful motivation scene.
Racial Synergies: Orc (+2 to physical domination and Flagellation scenes), Giant (+3 to intimidation and crushing restraint checks), Human (versatile +1 to any domination style).
Suggested Skills: Domination, Bondage, Flagellation, Negotiation/Trade, Appraisal.
Playstyle: You excel at breaking and shaping slaves, running breeding programs, and using erotic power as a tool of control and status.
**Erotic Courtesan / Pleasure Master Archetype**
Focus: Seduction, high-end pleasure services, and social manipulation through erotic skill.
Core Benefits: +2 to all Seduction and Erotic Performance checks. High Sexual Corruption grants +2 additional PP per scene. Easier reputation gains with decadent factions (Silken Collar, Silkveil, Velvet Rose). Once per session, convert a failed Seduction check into a partial success by spending a point of Sexual Corruption.
Racial Synergies: Elf (+3 to teasing/edging and elegant humiliation), Dryad (+2 to sensory and nature-infused scenes), Human (+1 to any seduction style).
Suggested Skills: Seduction, Oral (providing), Penile, Vaginal, Insight/Kink Discovery, Deception/Espionage.
Playstyle: You weaponize beauty, sensuality, and kink mastery to climb social ladders, gather secrets, and influence nobles through private performances.
**Crafter / Artificer Archetype**
Focus: Crafting high-value goods, custom restraints, vehicles, and erotic items.
Core Benefits: +2 to all Crafting checks (multi-skill projects). On-site crafting at Resource & Industrial properties grants +3 instead of +2. Masterwork items provide +10% reputation and profit multipliers. Once per session, reroll a failed crafting check when using custom erotic or restraint gear.
Racial Synergies: Dwarf (+3 to metalwork, blacksmithing, and engineering projects), Gnome (+3 to intricate gadgets and puzzle-like restraints), Goblin (+2 to experimental/chaotic inventions).
Suggested Skills: Any 12 core crafting skills (especially Engineering, Leatherwork, Tailoring, Carving, Blacksmithing), Appraisal.
Playstyle: You build your empire through workshops, custom erotic gear, and luxury goods that fuel trade and status displays.
**Raider / Smuggler Archetype**
Focus: Black market operations, wilderness raids, and high-risk trade runs.
Core Benefits: +2 to Stealth, Deception, and Vehicle Operation checks. Reduced Detection risk on black market actions (-4 instead of -2). Bonus loot and smuggling opportunities. Once per session, gain advantage on a smuggling or raid-related roll.
Racial Synergies: Orc (+2 to combat raids and intimidation during ambushes), Goblin (+3 to chaotic trickery and gadget-based smuggling), Human (+1 to versatile route planning).
Suggested Skills: Stealth/Infiltration, Negotiation/Trade, Survival skills (Foraging, Trapping, Camping), Combat skills.
Playstyle: You thrive on danger, smuggling forbidden goods or slaves, and turning raids into profit while navigating faction politics.
**Overseer / Estate Manager Archetype**
Focus: Property management, slave labor coordination, and daily estate routines.
Core Benefits: +2 to Administration and Overseer-related checks. Reduced Defiance drift on owned properties (-4 instead of -2 daily). Better passive income from estates and workshops (+15%). Once per week, reroll a failed overseer loyalty check.
Racial Synergies: Dwarf (+2 to industrial estate management and forge oversight), Human (+1 to balanced labor allocation), Orc (+2 to harsh but effective brute oversight).
Suggested Skills: Administration, Animal Handling/Beast Control, Insight, Negotiation, relevant crafting or survival skills.
Playstyle: You build long-term wealth through well-managed estates, loyal overseers, and efficient exploitation of land and labor.
**Mystic / Ritualist Archetype**
Focus: Rare magic use, enchanted items, and ritual enhancements.
Core Benefits: +2 to Arcana checks. Reduced backlash risk on minor rituals (-3 instead of standard). Access to enchanted restraints and body modifications. Once per session, reduce backlash severity on a failed ritual by one step.
Racial Synergies: Elf (+2 to Illusion and elegant ritual work), Dryad (+2 to nature/Alteration rituals), Human (+1 to versatile ritual adaptation).
Suggested Skills: Arcana, relevant Knowledge skills (Religion & Theology, Erotic & Forbidden Lore), Alchemy/Herbalism.
Playstyle: You wield dangerous magic to enhance slaves, create powerful items, or influence politics, always balancing power with the constant threat of backlash and church scrutiny.

**Breeder / Bloodline Architect Archetype** (New)
Focus: Generational breeding programs, pregnancy mastery, bloodline optimization, and legacy creation.
Core Benefits: +2 to all Pregnancy_Breeding_Rules and conception checks. High Sexual Corruption grants +3 to breeding success and hybrid trait rolls. Easier access to high-fertility slaves and bloodline alliances. Once per session, reroll a failed breeding or lactation roll.
Racial Synergies: Dryad (+3 to seasonal fertility rites and vine-bondage breeding), Elf (+2 to elegant multi-partner breeding scenes), Human (versatile +1 to any bloodline strategy).
Suggested Skills: Breeding, Lactation, Insight/Kink Discovery, Negotiation/Trade, Alchemy/Herbalism.
Playstyle: You build dynasties through calculated breeding, hybrid optimization, and generational power, turning slaves into living legacies while managing the grimdark risks of mutation and inheritance.

**Courtier / Faction Intriguer Archetype** (New)
Focus: Political maneuvering, reputation warfare, blackmail, and soft-power diplomacy.
Core Benefits: +2 to all Deception, Negotiation, and Faction Reputation checks. Luxury fashion and status displays grant +4 instead of +2 to social rolls. Once per session, convert a failed social check into a partial success by revealing a hidden secret or favor.
Racial Synergies: Elf (+3 to elegant court seduction and humiliation diplomacy), Human (versatile +1 to any intrigue style), Goblin (+2 to chaotic rumor-spreading and trickery).
Suggested Skills: Seduction, Deception/Espionage, Insight, Negotiation/Trade, Appraisal.
Playstyle: You climb through whispers, alliances, scandals, and erotic leverage, turning noble courts and faction summits into battlegrounds where pleasure and information are the ultimate weapons.

**Enforcer / Mercenary Commander Archetype** (New)
Focus: Combat security, slave enforcement, caravan protection, and violent reclamation of assets.
Core Benefits: +2 to all Combat and Intimidation checks involving slaves or raids. Reduced Defiance spikes during enforcement scenes. Once per session, gain advantage on a combat or rebellion-suppression roll when leading slaves or mercenaries.
Racial Synergies: Orc (+3 to brutal enforcement and flagellation intimidation), Giant (+2 to overwhelming physical dominance), Dwarf (+1 to fortified estate defense).
Suggested Skills: Combat skills, Domination, Bondage, Animal Handling/Beast Control, Vehicle Operation.
Playstyle: You maintain order through force, lead armed caravans, suppress rebellions, and reclaim escaped assets, turning violence and discipline into profitable empire-building tools.

Step 5: Choose Skills & Proficiencies
Use Skills_Master_List.txt v1.12 for the complete skill list and resolution mechanics.
- Choose 4–6 skills based on background and archetype (GM discretion). At character creation, distribute a total of 120 skill ranks across your chosen skills (maximum 40 in any single skill). Archetype-suggested skills should receive at least 20 ranks each for optimal play.
- Recommended archetype distributions (examples):
  - Dominant Owner: 30+ in Domination/Bondage/Flagellation, 20+ in Negotiation/Appraisal.
  - Erotic Courtesan: 30+ in Seduction/Erotic Performance skills, 20+ in Insight/Deception.
  - Crafter: 30+ in primary crafting skills, 20+ in Appraisal/Engineering.
  - Raider: 25+ in Stealth/Combat/Survival, 20+ in Negotiation.
  - Overseer: 30+ in Administration/Insight, 20+ in relevant crafting or handling skills.
  - Mystic: 30+ in Arcana, 20+ in Knowledge/Alchemy.
- Erotic Performance skills (Oral providing, Anal, Vaginal, Penile, Flagellation, Bondage) are available and highly relevant.
- Assign ranks (0–40 recommended at character creation).

**Time & Aging Considerations (New)**
All characters begin at a young adult age appropriate to their race (see Races_Reference.txt and Time_System.txt v1.1). During play, the player must track their character’s age using the Monthly Aging Check in Time_System.txt. Aging affects Body traits (especially Attractiveness, Endurance, and Strength), fertility, and social perception. Longevity potions or rituals (from Alchemy_Consumables_and_Potions.txt v1.5 and Magic_System.txt v1.13) can delay or manipulate aging but carry severe grimdark risks including rapid catch-up decay, mutations, Defiance spikes in slaves, and reputation loss. Starting characters are expected to be in their physical prime, but long campaigns will force meaningful choices about maintenance, alchemy, magic, and generational succession.

Step 10: Faction Backstories (Expanded)
Factions shape the world and your character’s opportunities. Choose or note starting reputation (usually Neutral or Unfriendly) and consider how your background and archetype tie into any faction.
Major Factions & Backstories:
- Iron Collar Guild: Professional slavers and trainers who control legal auctions. They rose during the 46 AE famine by organizing debt bondage contracts. They value efficiency, profitability, and well-trained slaves. Strong ties to crafting and trade.
- Verdant Consortium: Landowners and large-scale breeders who dominate agricultural and bloodline slavery. They emphasize “natural” ownership and generational stability. Close alliance with the Verdant Cycle church and dryad groves.
- Velvet Rose Society: Secretive network of dominants and pleasure-house operators who wield soft power through seduction and blackmail. They operate in the shadows of noble courts and pleasure districts.
- Freeborn League: Underground anti-slavery resistance born from famine survivors. They run safehouses, sabotage operations, and rescue missions. Highly active in border zones and slums.
- Silken Collar Church: Opulent, hedonistic faith that sanctifies erotic ownership and submission as divine gifts. Dominant in cosmopolitan areas; they bless high-end pleasure training and public displays.
- Broken Chain Church: Ascetic reformist faith that views slavery as a moral evil. They aid escapees and push for redemption. Growing influence among the poor and racial settlements.
- Hammer & Anvil Guild: Dominant crafting guild controlling weapons, vehicles, restraints, and workshops. They protect monopolies and supply the industrial backbone of the baronies.

Notes on Advancement:
- XP is earned through property upgrades, successful BDSM/breeding scenes, faction alliances, rebellion suppression, and major milestones (see Social_Advancement.txt v1.5).
- Ability Score Increases can be applied to any stat (max 20 unless magical enhancement is used).
- Feat slots: Racial feats are chosen at Level 1. General feats can be chosen from FEATS_MASTER_LIST.txt v1.0 or approved by GM.
- Archetype Features are gained automatically at the listed levels and stack with previous benefits.
- Social Tier progression remains separate and requires both XP thresholds and meeting advancement methods (property, erotic mastery, faction status, etc.).

Character Advancement
- Gain levels through play, major accomplishments, or significant property/wealth milestones.
- Each level grants +1 to one ability score or +5–10 skill ranks (GM discretion).
- New social standing tiers are earned through reputation, wealth, property, and demonstrated erotic or economic power (see Social_Advancement.txt).
Integration Notes
- All mechanics reference existing files: Body_Ranges_Reference, Skills_Master_List, Slavery_and_Defiance_Core_Mechanics, Sex_BDSM_System, etc.
- The Adult trait is mandatory for any erotic, breeding, or BDSM content.
- Starting characters are expected to be competent but not powerful — the game is designed for rags-to-riches growth through ownership and risk.
- Clothing, footwear, scent, and status display choices are guided by LUXURY_FASHION_AND_STATUS.txt v1.2 and affect early social rolls, Defiance drift, and public perception.
- Time and aging mechanics from Time_System.txt v1.1 must be tracked during long campaigns. Aging affects Body traits, fertility, endurance, and social standing. Alchemy longevity potions and magic rituals provide ways to delay decline but carry severe grimdark consequences (mutations, Defiance spikes, reputation loss).
- Feats are selected from FEATS_MASTER_LIST.txt v1.0 using the feat slots in the Character Advancement Table.
- All archetypes (now nine total) have full level-by-level feature progression that integrates with MAGIC_SYSTEM v1.13, FEATS_MASTER_LIST v1.0, and the new Character Advancement Table.
We will expand character creation options together (more backgrounds, racial feats, starting bloodlines, etc.) as needed.

CHARACTER EXPANSION & ENDGAME - Version 1.0 (Grimdark Erotic Fantasy, 70 AE)
THE AGING CRISIS (Post-Level 10 Mechanics)
When a character reaches Level 10, they must confront the Aging Crisis—the grim realization that mortality, decline, and irrelevance threaten everything they've built. This triggers immediately upon reaching Level 10 or when the character turns 50 years old (whichever comes first), and recurs every 5 years thereafter.

The Three Paths of Decline
At each Aging Crisis checkpoint, the player must choose one path. This choice is permanent for that 5-year period:

1. The Longevity Gamble (Magical Preservation) Postpone decay through dangerous alchemy and forbidden rituals

Mechanical Effect: Halts all physical aging for 5 years; retains current Body traits
The Cost: Roll on the Longevity Backlash Table (see below) immediately, and again at the end of the 5-year period
Social Impact: -2 Social Standing progress per use (visible "unnatural" aura); slaves gain +10 Defiance when witnessing the ritual (unnatural fear)
Requirements: Access to Alchemy_Consumables_and_Potions v1.5 (Youthful Vitality Tonic or better) OR Magic_System v1.13 (Eternal Youth Elixir Ritual, DC 32)
Longevity Backlash Table (d10) 1-2: Rapid Decay Cascade - Upon expiration of the 5 years, age 15 years instantly (triple trait loss) 3-4: Mutation Bloom - Gain 1d4 random Body trait shifts (±15 points, grotesque alterations per Medical_Trauma.txt) 5-6: Addiction to Youth - Permanent "Youth-Seeker" trait: Must consume 200gp worth of alchemical maintenance monthly or suffer -5 to all mental stats 7-8: Defiance Legacy - All offspring conceived during this period inherit +25 Defiance floor and "Corrupted Bloodline" trait (Legacy_Tracker v1.2) 9: Temporal Anchoring - Become unable to age naturally; if killed, crumble to dust (no resurrection possible via Necromancy) 10: The Portrait Effect - All physical damage transfers to a "soul portrait" (enchanted item); if portrait is destroyed, instant death. Portrait requires weekly blood tribute (1 HP).

2. Generational Hand-Off (Succession) Begin playing the heir, transitioning the founder to NPC status

Mechanical Effect: Create new Level 5 character (offspring or designated heir); Founder becomes "Patriarch/Matriarch" NPC with limited actions
Legacy Bonus: New character inherits 50% of parent's skill ranks and one Bloodline Trait (see Prestige Classes)
The Cost: Founder ages normally and loses 1d4 Physical Body traits permanently; -10 Defiance floor for all former slaves (grief/loyalty to dead/absent master)
Social Impact: +3 Social Standing progress (legitimate succession celebrated); -2 if hand-off was forced (rebellion)
Requirements: Valid heir from Pregnancy_Breeding_Rules v1.11 OR designated successor with 3+ years of Administer Estate training
3. The Frail Tyrant (Decline into Power) Embrace weakness as a different kind of strength

Mechanical Effect: Continue playing original character, but gain Fragile Condition: All physical stats capped at 12; -5 to all physical checks; +10 to Intimidation (fear of the dying tyrant); +20 to Defiance management rolls (slaves pity/fear the aging master)
Compensation: Unlock Wisdom of Ages - Double XP from teaching/training scenes; can cast Necromancy rituals without material components (using life force instead: -1 Con per ritual)
The Inevitability: Every 2 years, lose 1 point from Strength, Dexterity, or Constitution permanently. When any reaches 0, death occurs within 1d4 sessions.
Final Act: Upon death, may trigger Legacy Event - estate enters crisis mode (civil war among heirs) or Apotheosis (if prepared, become Undead Patriarch; see Ascension Rituals)
PRESTIGE CLASSES (Tier 5+ Archetypes)
Upon reaching Social Standing Tier 5 AND Level 10, characters may qualify for Prestige Classes. These replace standard archetype features with epic-scale abilities focused on legacy and empire.

1. THE BLOODLINE PATRIARCH / MATRIARCH
"My seed is my empire, my womb is the throne."

Requirements:

Social Standing Tier 5
Successfully bred 3+ generations of slaves (Legacy_Tracker v1.2)
Personal Sexual Corruption 60+ OR have offspring with 5+ different races
Estate includes Grand Breeding Sanctum or Enchanted/Forbidden Breeding Sanctum (Property_Ownership v1.9)
Features:

Level 11: Genetic Dominance

Offspring inherit +4 to two Body traits of your choice (instead of random)
Can perform Bloodline Purification: Spend 10,000 gp and 1 month to remove one Hybrid Trait drawback from your bloodline permanently
Grimdark Cost: Each purification ritual causes permanent -2 to your Constitution (genetic taxation)
Level 12: The Dynastic Will

Your bloodline becomes a tangible force. All slaves with your blood (even distant) suffer -10 Defiance when you are present (genetic awe)
New Action: Generational Command - Once per session, issue an order that propagates through all bloodline members within 10 miles; they automatically comply (no Defiance check) but gain +5 Insanity Points (Medical_Trauma.txt) from the psychic intrusion
Level 13: Living Legacy

If killed, you may possess the body of any willing bloodline descendant (including slaves you've bred) within 1 mile. New body has your mental stats but their physical stats (minus 2d6 permanent loss from the trauma of possession)
Limitation: Original body dies permanently; if no host available, true death
Level 14: The Bloodline Emperor

Create Bloodline Legion: Pool up to 50 bloodline slaves; they share a hive-mind Defiance score (average of all). If the group Defiance stays below 30, they fight with perfect coordination (+5 to combat rolls, no morale checks)
The Price: If any single member of the Legion reaches Defiance 76+, the entire Legion suffers a simultaneous Major Rebellion Chain (telepathic rage cascade)

Market Crash: Spend 50,000 gp to trigger an artificial Catastrophic Market Crash (Economy_Trade_System v1.26) in one specific category (e.g., pleasure slaves, iron ore). All rivals lose 50% income for 1d6 months; you gain their losses as profit
Grimdark Cost: Each crash causes 1d100 commoner deaths (famine/riot) and +20 Defiance for all slaves in the region (economic desperation)
Level 14: The Shadow Throne

Phantom Guild: Your black market contacts form a "shadow government" in one barony. You effectively control that barony's economy (set prices, embargo goods) but appear to be a humble merchant publicly
The Risk: If exposed, every faction turns hostile simultaneously (Hostile reputation with all legitimate groups); becomes most wanted criminal in the Principalities
